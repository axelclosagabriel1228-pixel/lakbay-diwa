Welcome to LAKBAY DIWA

Lakbay-Diwa is your space to discover, learn, and be empowered. Whether you're exploring leadership, voter education, or community involvement, this platform guides the youth toward making informed and impactful decisions.

Youth Empowerment

The youth are powerful catalysts for change. Here's what helps build their potential:

  • Access to quality information and education
  • Leadership development and life skills training
  • Active participation in community initiatives
  • Encouraging innovation and entrepreneurship

Voters Education

Understand your voice. Understand your power. Voting is more than a duty—it's a right and a responsibility.

  • Why your vote matters
  • How to register safely and correctly
  • Understanding the election process
  • Spotting misinformation and fake news

Get Involved

There are many ways to contribute to a better community:

  • Join youth programs and advocacy groups
  • Participate in local community work
  • Attend civic and leadership seminars
  • Encourage friends and family to vote
Your voice matters—start using it today.

Our Vision

To build an empowered generation of young Filipinos who are informed, confident, and actively engaged in shaping a just and progressive democratic future.

Our Mission

We aim to educate and inspire the youth through accessible and meaningful voter-empowerment initiatives; strengthen the voices of young citizens through platforms for dialogue, learning, and civic participation; and cultivate responsible, value-driven leaders who contribute to nation-building and informed democratic engagement.
